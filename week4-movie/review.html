REVIEW

Javier Almeida

April 20, 2025

Juice is a powerful story about friendship, loyalty, and the pressure to gain respect on the streets. The film shows how choices can change a person, especially when power gets in the way of trust. Tupac Shakur gives a deep, intense performance as Bishop.

I think Juice stands out because of how real and raw it feels. The music, setting, and acting all come together to show the struggles young men face when trying to define themselves. It's more than just a crime story â€” it's about identity, fear, and survival.
